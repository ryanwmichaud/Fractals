A real time fractal visualizer - draw a curve and see how it looks with each 'forward' segment replaced by itself! Or draw in real time at higher recursive depth!


W - Turn Up
A - Turn Left
S - Turn Down
D - Turn Right
P - Move Forward
Q - Turn Counterclockwise
E - Turn Clockwise


All actions including turns are recorded into the directions but you can use the undo button to undo your last action

scalce - tells us how you want your curve to scale down. For example with a scale of 3, each line will be 3 times smaller than the previous iteration. A curve that ends 3 units from the starting point will stay the same size!
depth tells us how many times to replace each forward segment with the entire curve
the direction string is a visual representation of the instructions for drawing the sdafasdgfb curve of the fractal. each f will be replaced with the entire formula
you can optionally edit or completly enter this string by hand - just hit the edit button to submit it!


f - Go forward
+ - Turn clockwise
- - Turn counter clockwise
n - Placeholder for redundant instructions
